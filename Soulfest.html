Soulfest





An annual music festival hosted by Newsound Concerts, Soulfest features a variety of Christain artists in a 3 day experience each August. To promote each year's festival, a new style guide is generated for the upcoming event which is used to create promotional media for the fest.




































Hannah created 8" by 10" posters advertising the daily lineup for the 3 day festival, highlighting the headliner for each day with an image of the artist(s). The back of each poster features some of the many activites the Soulfest experience entails. Muted hues of green, blue, purple, and red are used throughout the posters and following media to the grab attention of the majorly female audience. Drop shadows are also used throughout the Soulfest promotional material to add emphasis to the company logo, and any other highly important text on these saturated backgrounds. Sponsors and Soulfest contact formats are listed at the bottom for convenience.

































Using the similar composition to the posters above, Hannah generated Instagram and Facebook posts to promote the upcoming festival to Soulfest's social media followers. These posts were supplemented by a daily countdown to the fest beginning a week before opening day to hype up the fan base.





Hannah also created an ad featured in the Laconia Daily Sun paper the week before Soulfest to encourage the central New Hampshire community to attend the festival with their families.














During the weekend-long festival, brochures were presented to guests upon entry. Each brochure not only provided guests with a detailed lineup of artists at each of the 5 stages on campus, but also activites throughout the fest, and upcoming events hosted by Newsound Concerts.











For each day, Hannah color coded the days for ease in understanding the schedule. This color coding was carrier out on schedules posted throughout the campus. Additonally, several advertisements Hannah designed on the back of the brochure including the Merchandise Ad, Soul University, and Soulfest App Ad were made into banners. These banners were spread throughout the campus to ensure attendees were provided with ample knowledge of opportunities to further engage with Soulfest events.






























Additionally, slides were made to project at the largest stage during gaps between performances. Each slide promotes activites seen in the brochure at the fest, or advertising upcoming events headed by Soulfest.
