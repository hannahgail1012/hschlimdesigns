Speaker Series





This speaker series created to empower young female designers was looking for brochure design for their upcoming talk. Gathering skilled designers with CEO level positions in the field, this series was looking for a dynamic, modern design to intrigue young designers, prompting their visit to this talk. Use of a limited color palette and type-stacking were used to balance this contemporary aesthetic with clarity. *Note: The pages are listed chronologically as they would in the brochure.*
