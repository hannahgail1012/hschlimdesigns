About the Artist

Hannah is an artist based in New Hampshire studying Graphic Design at New England College. Her passion for design comes from an interest in hand lettering developed when she was young. In her work, she aims to combine this love of dynamic typography with organic shapes, and a clean, organized layout. With experience designing for clients ranging from a jewelry company to a music festival, her style is malleable, to allowing success for a wide variety of clients. Her current work focuses on promotional material and app design for a plant company dedicated to providing simple, accessible plant care for everyone.
